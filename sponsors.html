Call for Posters

The International Conference on Visualization and Computer-Human Interactions (VisCHI) is the first event of its kind in the region. It will bring together researchers and practitioners from the fields of Visualization and Human-Computer Interaction.

In many application domains, complex systems and data have to be analyzed and controlled through the collaboration between humans and machines. The goal of the conference is to support domain experts in these tasks, to expose, discuss and share new concepts, methods, and practices.

The VisCHI conference will allow sharing knowledge and practice between experts and practitioners by a set of keynote and invited talks by speakers from academia and industry.

The VisCHI organizing committee solicits posters and demonstrations from academia, industry, and government institutions to expose original work covering both theory and practice in the following fields:

  1. Data Visualization
  2. Information Visualization
  3. Scientific Visualization
  4. Visual Analytics
  5. Human-Computer Interaction
  6. User Experiments
  7. Explainable Artificial Intelligence
  8. User-Centric Design

or other fields concerned with techniques that support human-machine interactions to analyze and control complex systems such as: energy, security, education, and health.

The abstracts will be evaluated based on their novelty and relation to the topic of the event. Abstracts and final posters will be made available on the website of the conference.

Formatting Instruction: The poster/demo proposal should follow the template. The proposal should be sent to vischiqatar@gmail.com before August 30, 2019

Notification of acceptance/rejection will be sent by September 15, 2019.
