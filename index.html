CALL FOR POSTERS & DEMOS

The VisCHI organizing committee solicits posters and demonstrations from academia, industry, and government institutions to expose original work covering both theory and practice in the following fields:

  • Data Visualization
  • Information Visualization
  • Scientific Visualization
  • Visual Analytics
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • User Experiments
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • User-Centric Design
or other fields concerned with techniques that support human-machine interactions to analyze and control complex systems such as: energy, security, education, and health.

The abstracts will be made available on the website of the conference. Submission Guidelines
The poster or demo proposal should be one page abstract including title, author names, affiliation and coordinates, abstract, pictures and references.
The proposal should be sent to vischiqatar@gmail.com before August 30, 2019
Notification of acceptance/rejection will be sent by September 15, 2019.
